Get To Know

Tune

Lucki, born Lucki Camel Jr., is a rapper and producer from Chicago known for pioneering the underground wave of "alternative trap." Emerging in 2013 with his debut project Alternative Trap at just 17, Lucki quickly made a name for himself with introspective lyrics, hypnotic flows, and lo-fi production. His music blends raw emotion with hazy, drug-laced soundscapes, often touching on themes of addiction, fame, and isolation.

Over the years, Lucki has built a loyal fanbase without relying on major label push, releasing acclaimed projects like Freewave, Days B4 III, and Flawless Like Me. His ability to stay true to his sound while evolving with each release has earned him respect both underground and mainstream. More than just a rapper, Lucki represents a voice for a generation navigating numbness in a digital world—authentic, vulnerable, and always in his own lane.

Discography

Alternative Trap (2013)

Lucki’s debut mixtape, a defining release in the “alternative trap” movement. Raw, introspective, and ahead of its time.

Body High (2014)

A darker, moodier follow-up exploring themes of addiction and emotional distance.

Freewave (2015)

A lo-fi, stream-of-consciousness tape that further established Lucki’s signature sound.

Son of Sam (2016)

Minimalist production paired with deeply personal lyrics and a slowed-down delivery.

Watch My Back (2017)

One of Lucki’s most celebrated underground releases, filled with unfiltered emotion and melancholic beats.

Freewave 3 (2019)

A refined version of the Freewave series, this project showcased Lucki’s growth and deeper production choices.

Days B4 III (2019)

A fan favorite with a more polished sound and introspective lyricism.

Almost There (2020)

Short and focused, this tape gave fans an honest glimpse into Lucki’s mindset and struggle with fame.

Wake Up Lucki (2021)

A collaboration with producer F1lthy, blending chaotic rage-inspired beats with Lucki’s signature laid-back flow.

Flawless Like Me (2022)

Marked a new era in Lucki’s career, balancing mainstream reach with underground roots

S*x M*ney Dr*gs (2023)

Continued evolution with dreamlike production and deeper reflections on wealth, relationships, and loss.

GEMINI! (2024)

A standout EP that reflects Lucki’s dual nature—vulnerability vs. flex, numbness vs. clarity.
